!!!--- Permission.ps1 ---!!!
Code:		ps1-script_path\Permission.ps1 FolderPath
Example:	.\Permission.ps1 D:\Shared
==>> Get the permission rights of all the folders (files are not included and also sub-folders of it) located in D:\Shared
==>> Output will be in D:\Shared\Permission.csv
==>> ren D:\Shared\Permission.csv NewFile.csv

!!!--- Get-InstalledSoftware.ps1 ---!!!
Code:		ps1-script_path\Get-InstalledSoftware.ps1 HostName
Example:	.\Get-InstalledSoftware.ps1 BGPC000026050P
==>> Display all apps installed in the Computer

!!!--- Get-LocalGroupMembers.ps1 ---!!!
Code:		ps1-script_path\Get-LocalGroupMembers.ps1 HostName
Example:	.\Get-LocalGroupMembers.ps1 BGPC000026050P
==>> Display the members of Administrator and Remote Desktop Users of the Computer

!!!--- Get-LocalGroupMembers2.ps1 ---!!!
Code:		ps1-script_path\Get-LocalGroupMembers2.ps1 HostName 'LocalGroup'
Example:	.\Get-LocalGroupMembers2.ps1 BGPC000026050P 'Administrators'
Example:	.\Get-LocalGroupMembers2.ps1 BGPC000026050P 'Remote Desktop Users'
==>> Display the members of the localgroup of the Computer
